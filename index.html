About

Grapheme-color synaethesia is a common kind of synaethesia where people perceive colours when they see letters, numbers and some other symbols.

This is a very basic tool where you can see the colours as you type, as well as share and see yours and other people's colours.

Unfortunately I haven't yet made colours for words (e.g. Wednesday). That will come at some point.
